Plai24

Tags: Exhibition - Visual identity

Tools: After effects - MAX 8 - Touchdesigner

NABA presents "PLAI," an exhibition-playground showcasing the Academy's creative media production. Dive into a dynamic space where creativity and innovation converge, highlighting the diverse and imaginative works of our talented students. I was responsible for the visual identity of the exhibition, adopting a playful approach by creating a series of generative typography experiments in TouchDesigner. This resulted in a vibrant and engaging visual experience that effectively reflects the innovative spirit of the academy.

Collaborators: Federico Ruggeri, Stefania Orlando

Documentation: Claudia Santillan

Generative Typography

Developed in TouchDesigner, this procedural typography system powered PLAI24's visual identity across digital and physical media. Modular algorithms generated evolving letterforms, each iteration unique yet coherent for social assets and large format posters. Generative Sound

I created the sound component for PLAI24 using Max 8 and BEAP modular synthesis tools. The system generates evolving soundscapes in real time, working independently from the visual elements to form an immersive experience. By focusing on generative synthesis, I aimed to keep the sound organic and dynamic, fitting the exhibition's playful tone while maintaining a cohesive feel across the space. I also adapted the audio for social media to ensure a consistent sound across different platforms.
