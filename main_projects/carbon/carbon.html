Carbon

Tags: Motion - 3D - BioInspired - Advertisement

Tools: Houdini - After Effects - Premiere

A conceptual social media campaign designed as an assignment to introduce CARBON, an Italian cosmetics brand using activated charcoal for skin purification. The visual narrative contrasts the roughness of charcoal with the smoothness of refined skin: rocks tumbling against weightless, fluid fabrics. A powerful soundtrack drives dynamic scene transitions, with layered sound effects enhancing key moments. More than just a product promotion, this project explores brand identity, authenticity, and sensory storytelling.
