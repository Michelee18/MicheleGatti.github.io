Genesi

Tags: Motion - 3D - BioInspired - Nature

Tools: Houdini - After Effects - Premiere

The Genesi campaign is a thesis study on communicating cultural institutions, it explores how a natural science museum can engage audiences. Through three 30-second videos on mineralogy, botany, and mycology, the campaign connects viewers to the museum’s digital resources while highlighting nature’s influence on design and artistic inspiration.

Supervisor: Roberto Bonisoli

Sound: Juergen branz - Sound pack for motion designer

Outcome

The thesis developed a research framework integrating museology, digital design theory, and preproduction methodology. The written documentation systematically analyzed how these disciplines intersect, establishing a structured approach to cultural communication. It detailed the application of museological principles to digital media design while documenting the preproduction process. The outcome was a rigorous methodological model for bridging academic research with digital cultural production.
